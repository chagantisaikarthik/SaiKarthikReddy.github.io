About Me

With a solid foundation in mechanical engineering from Koneru Lakshmaiah Education Foundation, India, I have developed expertise in SolidWorks Mechanical Design, FEA, and programming with Python. My engineering skills are complemented by a project management certification from Google, showcasing my ability to lead and complete projects efficiently. My professional experience includes serving as a Technical Inspector at ISIE, where I ensured the safety compliance of vehicles and designed challenging tracks. My hands-on internships with Southcentral Railways offered me invaluable experience in maintenance and safety protocols. Notably, I led my team to 1st place in the Quad Bike Design Challenge 2020, demonstrating my leadership and innovative design skills. Projects

Project 1

Designing of Quad Bike

❮ ❯

As the team leader(Team Unblockabulls) for the Quad Bike Design Challenge 2020, I spearheaded our efforts in engineering a high-performance quad bike, leading us to secure the 1st position among 73 competing teams across India. This project was a testament to our team's innovation, technical prowess, and collaborative efforts.

TECHNICAL CONTRIBUTIONS:
Braking System Design: Directed the team's design efforts, focusing on the vehicle's braking system to ensure safety and reliability under various operating conditions.
SolidWorks Design & Analysis: Leveraged SolidWorks to custom-design a brake rotor (disc), incorporating specific geometries and materials tailored for optimal braking performance. Utilized Ansys for thermal analysis, ensuring the rotor's performance remained uncompromised under high stress and temperature conditions.
Differential Gearbox & Wheel Hub: Engineered a bespoke Differential Gearbox and Wheel Hub, focusing on maximizing power transmission efficiency and vehicle stability. These components were precision-machined in CNC using high-grade Aluminum, ensuring durability and lightweight construction.

chasis design for Baja SAE

This project was not just a testament to mechanical engineering principles but also an exploration into the integration of modern design and manufacturing techniques.

Material Innovation: A pivotal aspect of the project was exploring and adapting the use of Aluminum for the vehicle's parts. Engineering and Impact:
This project was a comprehensive exercise in mechanical design, engineering principles, and innovative material use. By designing the chassis and components in SolidWorks and navigating the complexities of CNC machining with Aluminum, the project underscored the importance of integrating design tools and manufacturing techniques for advanced vehicle engineering
. The successful fabrication of the off-road car not only demonstrated technical proficiency but also paved the way for future projects to explore the potential of lightweight materials and advanced design methodologies in automotive engineering.
